About Peace Cleaners

Peace Cleaners started with a simple idea: offer honest cleaning that people can trust. We’re a small local company serving families, businesses, landlords, and property managers in Grande Prairie. Our goal is to make every space cleaner, healthier, and more comfortable.

We offer flexible scheduling, same-day service when available, and clear pricing with no surprises. We bring our own supplies and equipment.
